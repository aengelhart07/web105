Pyramids

Great Pyramid of Giza

The largest and oldest of the pyramids.
Also known as the Pyramid of Khufu.
The pyramid was the tallest man-made structure in the world for 3,800 years.

Pyramid of Khafre

The second largest pyramid.
Khafre's pyramid sits on top of 10m bedrock so it appears taller than Khufu's pyramid.
Usually associated with the Great Sphinx of Giza.

Pyramid of Menkaure

The smallest of the main pyramids.
Attempt to demolish the pyramids started with Menkaure. It left a vertical slash at it's northern face

The Pyramids of Giza

The pyramids are located in Greater Cairo, Egypt. The site is at the edge of the Western Desert.
The Great Pyramids of Giza is the oldest of the Seven Wonders of the Ancient World.
The Giza Pyramid Complex can also be called Giza Necropolis. The three main pyramids and surrounding structures.
