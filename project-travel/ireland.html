Ireland

Northern Ireland

Part of the United Kingdom.

Its capital is Belfast. Second largest city on the island.

Sites to see include Giants Causeway, Carrick-A-Rede Bridge, and the Dark Hedges.


Republic of Ireland

Officially named Ireland.

Its capital is Dublin. 40% of population lives in Greater Dublin Area. It is the largest city on the island.

Some castles to visit Bunratty, Ballymoon, Dunguaire, Dunluce, and Huntington.
