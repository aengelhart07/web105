Untitled Goose Game

By House House

Untitled Goose Game is a simple game about a goose being a nuisance while completing goals.
How much of a silly goose can you be?

It's a lovely morning in the village, and you are a horrible goose.
The pure quote by creators efficiently set the mood of the game style.

Overview

In September of 2019, developed by House House and published by Panic, Untitled Goose Game was released. The art style is minimalistic. The soundtrack is melodic piano filled. This is a puzzle-based game that relies on stealth. All together making the game gentle and relaxing. It became a major hit, winning awards and being nominated for many more.

In a decent sized English village, the player runs around as a chaotic goose. The goose can run, duck, flap wings, interact with objects using beak, and of course, honk. Each section of town has its own challenges and features. There are plenty of characters and objects to interact with.

Some of the fun is startling the people, some is theft. Tasks include annoying a poor gardener, agitating neighbors, and disturbing a pub. It is all about the chase.

Opinion

Untitled Goose Game is simplistic yet entertaining.

It is simply fun. It makes being the bad guy enjoyable. The tasks are silly, and the end game is a speed challenge. It leaves room for adventure and new ways to bother the neighborhood. Overall, the game is a great and relaxing.

The only downside is the short playthrough, but finishing a game does not mean that playing it stops.

Update

One year after the release, coming in a month, there will be an addition to the game. Co-op will be coming to Untitled Goose Game. A new Chinese goose will be added with red bill and legs. This will allow two players to team up and take on the town.
